# Clinical Note Template

## CHIEF COMPLAINT
A brief statement describing the primary reason for the patient’s visit, expressed in the patient’s own words when possible.

---

## HISTORY OF PRESENT ILLNESS
A chronological narrative describing the development of the patient’s current condition, including:
- Onset and duration of symptoms
- Severity and progression
- Associated symptoms
- Relevant contextual factors (travel, activity, stress)

---

## REVIEW OF SYSTEMS
A system-by-system review documenting positive and negative findings as reported by the patient.

---

## PHYSICAL EXAMINATION
Objective findings observed by the clinician during the physical examination.

---

## RESULTS
Relevant diagnostic findings including laboratory results, imaging, and other investigations.

---

## ASSESSMENT AND PLAN
A structured summary of clinical reasoning, including:
- Assessment of the patient’s conditions
- Medical reasoning supporting the assessment
- Treatment plan
- Follow-up recommendations
- Preventive or maintenance care
